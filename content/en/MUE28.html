MÜ 28

 

1997 MUE 28, profile Ritz 1, 2,5 m wing span, 3,6 kg mass

2002, MÜ 28, I build new wings with a RG 15 as profile, 2,58 m wing span, this time I added extra flapperons which allows more uncritical slow flight.
The wings are attached through 15 mm round steel used as ballast to get a mass of 4,5 kg. With this weight the glider has real good performance good weather and a breeze as prerequisite.



As usual I bought the fuselage at Gewalt Modellbau and build the wings by my own, Styrofoam covered with Abachi. I can use this glider up to a wind velocity of 50 km/h (measured at my house top).

The plan for the modified wings are constructed and drawn using the free available 2D CAD program Solid Edge. (MUE28_Fluegel.pdf).
