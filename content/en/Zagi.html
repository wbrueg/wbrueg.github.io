Zagi

2003, Zagi, profil ZAGI, according a drawing downloaded from Internet, 1 m wing span.



From time to time I need to build some flying vehicle. Triggered by a colleague I want to build some thing with very less effort. According the plan I cut the wings from Styrodur. As target the glider should fit into a backpack, therefore the wing where slitted into 2 attachable pieces.

The vacation time comes closer, so the building time comes under pressure. To save time I covers the cut wings with 110 gr. glass with Epoxy. Coated with filler, grind and paint with red color, the ears are fixed with double sticky tape and a plastic screw.

With this glider the typical "duck wiggle" appears, so the v-form of the wings should better on the negative side. As variation the Zagi is OK, but it is to small, flying far away isn't possible. To make some funny figures close by it's perfect. Due to the material the Zagi get slide damaged each landing, may be its better to use EPP next time.
